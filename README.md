# 2048-game

What's included:

Smooth tile animations — slide, merge pop, and new tile scale-in effects
Undo — press Z or tap the Undo button to revert your last move
Best score persistence — your high score is saved via localStorage
Win & Game Over detection — with the option to keep playing past 2048
Swipe support — fully playable on mobile with touch gestures
Move counter — tracks total moves made
Score pop animation — shows +points when you merge tiles
Dark neon aesthetic — gradient glow tiles, ambient background, polished typography
